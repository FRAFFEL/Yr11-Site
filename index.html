Definitions

The term Deep web and Dark web are two different services but many people confuse them and use them interchangeably here is the difference.

Deep Web
The Deep Web is what you can’t directly access on the internet by tradition means such as a basic search engine such as Google or Bing. This include many networks, and private information from government information, medical records to anonymous forums and drug deals.

Dark Web
The Dark web is a part of the Deep Web that is made of small peer-to-peer networks but also has a few larger networks such as Tor and Freenet. They can allow anonymous website hosting. This anonymity means these websites can be used for illegal activities. - James S.

Drugs on the Dark Web

Illicit and Prescription drugs is the biggest category within the dark web. Approximately 15.4% of all hidden services (dark web/ deep web websites/ online services) are drug related. The most notorious illicit drug website is called the Silk Road. This website is based on the transportation and smuggling of drugs to people who order them. Websites like these use a cryptocurrency called Bitcoin. Drugs are able to reach across the globe sometimes spanning all the way around the globe to meet its final destination. Obviously, the further these substances are sent the higher the risk of authorities seizing the substances. The Silk Road, and many other websites transporting and selling illegal and prescription drugs take advantage of the postage service and system, hiding these dangerous substances from chocolate bars to other miscellaneous items to pass through countries postage security measures to reach the anonymous customer.- Jaike R.

What Are Authorities are Doing

The governments of countries such as the United States of America and Australia are working towards bringing down websites containing illegal markets that are selling and purchasing illicit goods and performing illegal services (such as hacking, hitmen, selling of drugs etc.). The FBI shut down an infamous drug trading website by the name of the Silk Road in October 2013, however the website still to this day remains online by bringing the website up whenever it goes down. When drugs are posted authorities are able to search suspicious packages that may contain illegal items and are sometimes able to intervene a drug trade, then the drugs are destroyed. Enforcement bodies (such as the Police and FBI etc.) are able to find people purchasing drugs online as well as selling drugs on the dark web. These enforcement bodies then arrest the accused person and search their computers and other devices, as well as their home if suspected of selling or purchasing illicit substances.- Jaike R.

Dark Web Hackers

Hacking Services are highly sought after in the Dark Web. Hackers on the deep web can be hired to conduct a realistic penetration test or alternatively they can be hired to take control of a Gmail or Facebook account, most likely for cyber espionage. According to Business Insider it will cost $90 to hack into a Gmail account, whereas it will cost $350 to hack into someone’s Facebook account. Hackers can also attack a person’s Netflix account for only $1.25. There are other Hacking services available in the deep web, for example, you are able to take hacking courses for $20, it is also possible to ask for a hit and run attack, and for example a DDoS attack or you can request website defacement. The majority of hackers on the Deep Web are a part of a community which are closed to the public, and to enter you must request an invitation to join these hacking groups in discussion. In many cases these groups are specialized in their activities, as they target specific topics and or practices, which for example can include Social media hacking or data theft. - Flynn G.

How Tor Works

Tor is a series of networks used to browse the dark web. Tor does not directly connect the client to the server but pings the client between multiple server around the world to ensure anonymity. For example a user from Australian when using Tor may have their connection routed through America, Europe and then Asia to be connected to the server they wish. This is why the Tor logo is a onion as it has many layers. - James S.
